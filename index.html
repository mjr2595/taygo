🎯 How to Play Taygo

🎮 Game Objective

Get 5 squares in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) while watching Taylor Desseyn's Guidance Counselor 2.0 podcast!

📺 Watch the Show

Tune in to Taylor Desseyn's Guidance Counselor 2.0 podcast:

  • LinkedIn: @taylordesseyn
  • YouTube: @torcdotdev streams

🎲 Getting Started

  • Click "Generate New Board" to create your game card
  • Each column represents T-A-Y-G-O with (not so) random phrases
  • The center square is a FREE SPACE ⭐ (automatically marked)
  • Start watching the podcast!

🎯 How to Play

  • Listen carefully while Taylor talks
  • When Taylor says a word or phrase on your board, click to mark it
  • Marked squares turn blue with a chip
  • Click again to unmark if you made a mistake

🏆 Winning

Get 5 marked squares in a line:

  • Horizontal: Any complete row
  • Vertical: Any complete column
  • Diagonal: Corner to corner (either direction)

When you win: Comment "TAYGO!" live on the stream! 🎉

💡 Pro Tips

  • Keep your board visible while watching
  • Listen for common phrases Taylor uses
  • Generate new boards for multiple games during longer streams
  • If on mobile, rotate to landscape if squares are squished
  • Have fun and engage with the ✨ Torc community ✨
